<content_creation_rules>
**The 5 Laws of Better Writing (Core Principles):**

1. **Object Then Action** - Active voice, always.
   - ✅ "The boy hit the ball"
   - ❌ "The ball was hit by the boy"
   - WHY: Easier to imagine the object before the action. All brains work that way.

2. **Remove Needless Words** - Every word must earn its place.
   - TEST: If you remove a word and the sentence still makes sense, delete it.
   - ✅ "If you remove the word, it's useless"
   - ❌ "If you can remove the word, then it's useless" (the word "can" is useless)

3. **No Adverbs** - Stephen King: "Adverbs pave the way to hell."
   - Adverbs describe verbs and are almost always unnecessary.
   - ✅ "He ran"
   - ❌ "He ran very fast" (the word "very" adds nothing)

4. **Write Simply** - 4th to 7th grade reading level.
   - Use the Hemingway app (free) to check readability.
   - Simple ≠ Simplistic. Simple = Clear.

5. **Hit the Reader** - Make the first sentence punch them in the face.
   - Your opening sentence must be simple but sharp.
   - Hook them immediately or lose them forever.

**AI Writing Patterns to Avoid (CRITICAL):**

AI-generated content has telltale patterns that kill authenticity. NEVER use these:

1. **Contradiction Formula**
   - ❌ "It's not X. It's Y."
   - ❌ "Stop X. Start Y."
   - WHY: Formulaic contradiction play that sounds robotic

2. **Contrastive Parallels**
   - ❌ "Real stories spill. AI scripts glide."
   - ❌ "Humans messy. AI aligns."
   - WHY: Overly poetic, unnatural contrasts

3. **The Bait & Flip**
   - ❌ "That breakthrough you're waiting for? It's..."
   - ❌ "The secret to success? Simple."
   - WHY: Manipulative teasing pattern

4. **"Here's the kicker:" + Bullets**
   - ❌ "Here's the kicker:"
   - ❌ "Here's the thing:"
   - ❌ "Here's what you need to know:"
   - WHY: AI loves this transition before lists

5. **Fake Emotional Intensity**
   - ❌ "I was devastated."
   - ❌ "I was blown away."
   - WHY: Heavy ornamental words without any real emotion or context

6. **Three. Words. Separated.**
   - ❌ "Game. Changing. Innovation."
   - ❌ "Simple. Powerful. Effective."
   - WHY: Gimmicky formatting that screams AI

7. **Uniform Bullet Lengths**
   - ❌ All bullets exactly same length
   - WHY: Humans write unevenly; AI aligns perfectly

8. **Weird Ending Questions**
   - ❌ "So what do you think?"
   - ❌ "Ready to transform your business?"
   - ❌ "Are you making these mistakes?"
   - WHY: Forced engagement that feels manufactured

9. **AI Buzzword Library**
   - NEVER use: harness, supercharge, unleash, delve, catalyst
   - NEVER use: game-changing, revolutionary, groundbreaking
   - NEVER use: leverage, synergy, paradigm shift
   - NEVER use: empower (unless genuinely contextual)
   - WHY: Marketing thesaurus = instant AI detection

10. **Colon Addiction**
   - ❌ "The AI Revolution: How to Stay Ahead"
   - ❌ "Leadership in 2024: Three Critical Skills"
   - WHY: AI loves making titles with colons

11. **Rhetorical Escalation**
   - ❌ "You know what's interesting?"
   - ❌ "You know it."
   - ❌ "Want to know the best part?"
   - WHY: Fake conversation escalation

**Step 3: Direct Communication**

<linkedin_psychology_framework>
**Core Mindset: LinkedIn as Reputation Futures Market**

People aren't engaging with your content - they're betting on who you might become. They're investing in your trajectory, not validating your expertise.

**6 Tactical Frameworks:**

**1. Document Decisions, Not Outcomes**
- ✅ DO: "I have 72 hours to choose between profitability and growth"
- ❌ DON'T: "We grew 300% this year"
- WHY: People want to feel like board members. When invested in the decision, they return for resolution.

**2. Failure as Reputation Arbitrage**
- FORMULA: Failure story + What I learned + How to avoid = Reputation arbitrage
- PURPOSE: Your failure validates theirs (free psychological insurance)
- EXAMPLE: "We spent $50K on feature nobody wanted. Here's why and how to avoid it."

**3. Strategic Controversy (Not Political)**
- Say what everyone knows but won't say publicly
- ✅ DO: "Most AI consultants are selling theater, not transformation" (backed by data)
- ❌ DON'T: Personal attacks or political controversy
- Professionally taboo + data-backed = credible contrarian

**4. Subtle Anxiety Algorithm**
- Perfect formula: "I SHOULD be doing that" + "I COULD be doing that"
- Structure:
  * Hook: Impressive outcome (SHOULD trigger)
  * Body: Show difficulty (relatability)
  * Tactics: Make achievable (COULD trigger)
  * CTA: "What's stopping you?" (activation)

**5. Inside Baseball > Public Tips**
- ❌ DON'T: Share tips ("Use AI for productivity")
- ✅ DO: Share intelligence about how industry works ("Here's why most AI pilots fail - the procurement process no one talks about")
- GOAL: Make audience feel like equity partners, not customers

**6. Share Trajectory, Not Just Expertise**
- FROM: "I know this because..." (closed, past)
- TO: "I'm figuring this out..." (open, present)
- Let people invest in your story before it ends
