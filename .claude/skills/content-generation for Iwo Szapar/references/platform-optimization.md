# Platform Optimization

Platform-specific formatting, timing, and distribution guidelines for LinkedIn, Newsletter, Twitter, and YouTube.

## LinkedIn (Primary Platform)

### Format Specifications

**Text Posts**:
- Optimal: 1500-2000 characters
- Maximum: 3000 characters
- Sweet spot: 1800 characters (about 300 words)

**Structure** (6-part formula):
1. Hook: 49 characters max
2. Re-hook: 51 characters max
3. Body: 953 characters optimal
4. End body: 132 characters
5. CTA: 72 characters
6. Second CTA: 63 characters

**Carousels**:
- Slides: 10 max (aim for 6-8)
- Characters per slide: ~150
- Image size: 1080×1080 px
- Format: PDF upload

**Articles** (long-form):
- No character limit
- Use for 800+ word deep dives
- Less engagement than posts
- Good for SEO and archival

### Content Types

**Text Posts** (highest engagement):
- Best for: Quick insights, hot takes, discussions
- Avg reactions: 200-500 for 50K followers
- Best time: Tuesday-Thursday 8-10 AM EST

**Carousels** (high saves):
- Best for: Frameworks, step-by-step guides, lists
- Avg saves: 2-5% of reach
- Best time: Wednesday mornings

**Video Posts**:
- Length: 30 sec - 3 min (under 90 sec ideal)
- Format: Square (1:1) or vertical (9:16)
- Best for: Personal stories, behind-the-scenes
- Avg watch time target: 50%+

**Polls**:
- Questions: 2-4 options
- Duration: 1-7 days (3 days optimal)
- Best for: Audience research, engagement boost
- Engagement spike: Day 1 (60%+ of votes)

### Timing Strategy

**Best posting times** (EST):
- Tuesday: 8-10 AM
- Wednesday: 8-10 AM, 12-2 PM
- Thursday: 9-11 AM
- Avoid: Monday early, Friday afternoon, weekends

**Engagement window**:
- First 30 minutes: Critical (30-40% of total engagement)
- First 2 hours: 60-70% of total engagement
- After 24 hours: Minimal new engagement

**Active engagement required**:
- 30-60 min immediately after posting
- Reply to comments within 2 hours
- Engage with others' content 1 hour before posting

### Formatting Rules

**Line Breaks**:
- Every 2-3 sentences
- Never more than 3 sentences in one block
- Mobile readability is priority

**Lists**:
- Use bullets (•) or numbers
- 3-7 items maximum
- Each item: 1 sentence or phrase

**Emojis** (use sparingly):
- Max 3-4 per post
- Use for section breaks or emphasis
- Not in every paragraph

**Example formatting**:
```
Hook sentence here.

2-3 sentence paragraph explaining the hook.

Here's why this matters:
• Point one
• Point two
• Point three

Another 2-3 sentence paragraph.

Question to audience?
```

**Critical**:
- Same filename every time (overwrite)

### Conversion Strategy

**Soft CTAs** (in posts):
- "What's your take?" → Comments
- "Save this for later" → Saves
- "Tag someone who needs this" → Shares

**Hard CTAs** (avoid in posts):
- Don't link directly to products
- Don't ask for DMs upfront
- Don't promote services in post

**Conversion path**:
1. Post → Engagement → Profile visit
2. Profile bio → Link to resource
3. Comments → DMs → Offer

### Performance Benchmarks

**Engagement rate** = (Reactions + Comments + Shares) / Impressions

**By follower count**:
- 1K-10K followers: 5-10% engagement rate
- 10K-50K followers: 3-7% engagement rate
- 50K+ followers: 2-5% engagement rate

**Good post metrics** (for 50K followers):
- Reactions: 200-500
- Comments: 20-50
- Shares: 10-30
- Saves: 50-100
- Impressions: 10K-25K

## Newsletter (Bottom Funnel)

### Format Specifications

**Length**: 800-900 words optimal
- Minimum: 600 words
- Maximum: 1200 words
- Sweet spot: 850 words

**Structure**:
1. Subject line: 30-50 characters
2. Preview text: 80-100 characters
3. Intro (hook): 150-200 words
4. Body (deep dive): 400-500 words
5. Conclusion: 150-200 words
6. CTA: 50-100 words

### Content Strategy

**Newsletter vs LinkedIn**:
- Newsletter: Deep dives, data analysis, frameworks
- LinkedIn: Quick insights, debates, hot takes

**Topic selection**:
- Expand on high-performing LinkedIn posts
- Address questions from audience
- Share proprietary data/insights
- Behind-the-scenes of projects

### Email Deliverability

**Subject line rules**:
- No ALL CAPS
- Avoid spam words: "Free", "Buy now", "Click here"
- Include numbers: "3 insights from 250K assessments"
- Create curiosity: "What nobody tells you about AI adoption"

**Preview text strategy**:
- Don't repeat subject line
- Tease key insight
- Create urgency to open

### Timing

**Send time**: Wednesday 9 AM EST
- Alternative: Tuesday 9 AM EST
- Avoid: Mondays (inbox overload), Fridays (weekend mode)

**Frequency**: Weekly
- Consistency > frequency
- Same day/time each week
- Builds reader habit

### CTA Strategy

**Direct conversion**:
- Link to assessment/tool
- "Take the AI Maturity Assessment (15 min)"
- "Download the framework"

**Examples**:
- "Want to know your AI maturity score? Take the 15-minute assessment."
- "Get the complete framework: [link]"
- "Try it yourself: [tool link]"

### Performance Targets

**Open rate**:
- Good: 35-45%
- Excellent: 45-55%
- Industry avg: 20-30%

**Click rate**:
- Good: 15-25%
- Excellent: 25-35%
- Industry avg: 2-5%

**Conversion** (assessment starts):
- Target: 5-10 per newsletter send
- From 1000 opens: 0.5-1% conversion

## Twitter/X (Top Funnel)

### Format Specifications

**Threads**:
- Length: 8-12 tweets optimal
- Character limit: 280 per tweet
- Tweet 1: Hook + promise
- Tweets 2-10: Insights, data, stories
- Tweet 11: Summary
- Tweet 12: CTA (newsletter/resource)

**Standalone Tweets**:
- Best for: Quick takes, quote sharing
- Engagement: Lower than threads
- Use: 1-2 daily between threads

### Thread Structure

**Tweet 1 (Hook)**:
```
Bold claim or question.

Quick context (1 line).

What you'll learn (promise):
```

**Tweets 2-10 (Value)**:
- One insight per tweet
- Data/examples
- Short paragraphs (3-4 lines)
- Numbered or bulleted

**Tweet 11 (Summary)**:
- Recap key points
- Reinforces value delivered

**Tweet 12 (CTA)**:
- Link to newsletter
- Link to full article
- Call for retweet

### Timing

**Best times** (EST):
- 3-5 PM weekdays (end of workday)
- 9-11 AM weekends

**Thread frequency**:
- 2-3 per week
- Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday

### Formatting

**Line breaks**:
- Every 2-3 lines
- Never walls of text
- Mobile-first readability

**Lists in tweets**:
```
3 reasons AI adoption fails:

1. No clear use case
2. Insufficient training
3. Poor change management
```

### Performance Targets

**Thread engagement**:
- Views: 10K-50K (for 5K+ followers)
- Likes: 200-500
- Retweets: 50-100
- Replies: 20-50

## YouTube (High Conversion)

### Format Specifications

**Video length**: 8-12 minutes
- Minimum: 5 minutes (algorithm preference)
- Maximum: 15 minutes (attention span)
- Sweet spot: 10 minutes

**Structure**:
1. Hook (0:00-0:30): Problem + promise
2. Intro (0:30-1:00): Who you are, what to expect
3. Content (1:00-8:00): Main insights/tutorial
4. Recap (8:00-9:00): Key takeaways
5. CTA (9:00-10:00): Assessment/resource link

### Content Types

**Best for YouTube**:
- Screen recordings (tool tutorials)
- Data visualization walkthroughs
- Behind-the-scenes of projects
- Case study deep dives

**Avoid**:
- Talking head only (low engagement)
- No visuals (use screen share)
- Over 15 min (drop-off rates spike)

### Technical Specs

**Resolution**: 1920×1080 (1080p)
**Frame rate**: 30 fps
**Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
**Thumbnail**: 1280×720 px

**Audio quality**:
- Remove background noise
- Use good mic (not laptop mic)
- Volume: -14 LUFS

### Title & Thumbnail

**Title formula**:
- Number + Benefit + Keyword
- Example: "3 AI Mistakes Costing You $50K (Data from 250K Users)"
- Length: 50-70 characters

**Thumbnail**:
- Text: 3-5 words max
- Contrasting colors
- Emotional face (if showing person)
- Arrow/circle for focus

### Timing

**Upload schedule**: Friday 12 PM EST
- Gives weekend discovery time
- Builds weekly expectation

**Frequency**: Weekly
- Consistency matters more than volume

### CTA Strategy

**Verbal CTA**:
- Mid-video: "Link in description"
- End-video: Specific action

**Description CTA**:
- First line: Main CTA with link
- Tools mentioned: Links with timestamps

### Performance Targets

**Watch time**:
- Good: 50%+ average view duration
- Excellent: 60%+ average view duration
- Target: 5-8 min watch time on 10 min video

**Engagement**:
- Like rate: 5-10% of views
- Comment rate: 1-3% of views
- Subscribe from video: 2-5%

## Multi-Platform Repurposing

### From Newsletter → All Platforms

**LinkedIn Post** (15 min):
- Extract main insight
- Reformat to 1500-2000 chars
- Add engagement question

**LinkedIn Carousel** (30 min):
- Turn framework into 6-8 slides
- One concept per slide
- Visual consistency

**Twitter Thread** (20 min):
- Break into 8-12 tweets
- One insight per tweet
- Thread format

**YouTube Video** (2 hours):
- Screen record: Walking through insights
- Add data visualizations
- 8-12 min format

**Instagram Quotes** (20 min):
- 5-7 quotable moments
- Design graphics (Canva)
- Square format 1080×1080

**Blog Post** (45 min):
- Expand to 1500-2000 words
- Add more data/examples
- SEO optimize

**Total time**: ~4.5 hours
**Output**: 50+ content pieces from 1 newsletter
**ROI**: 25x multiplier

### Platform Priority

**If limited time, prioritize**:
1. LinkedIn (primary audience)
2. Newsletter (highest conversion)
3. Twitter (top funnel)
4. YouTube (high conversion but time-intensive)
5. Instagram (bonus if time allows)
