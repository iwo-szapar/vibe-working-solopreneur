# Validation System

3-part evaluation system scoring content 0-100 before publishing. Based on analysis of 6 top LinkedIn creators with combined 3.7M+ followers.

## Composite Scoring Formula

**Final Score** = (Hook × 0.40) + (Archetype × 0.25) + (Engagement × 0.35)

**Thresholds**:
- **85-100**: Viral potential → Publish immediately
- **70-84**: High quality → Publish with confidence
- **50-69**: Good → Minor tweaks needed
- **Below 50**: Major revision or skip

## Evaluation 1: Viral Hook Pattern (0-100)

Analyzes first 1-2 lines against 10 proven viral patterns.

### The 10 Patterns (Ranked by Avg Engagement)

1. **Visual Meme/Humor** (500-1000+ reactions)
   - "How I feel about X..." (implies visual)
   - Emoji-driven opening
   - Cultural reference

2. **Contrarian Statement** (400+ reactions)
   - "Everyone's doing X wrong"
   - "Stop doing Y"
   - Challenges mainstream

3. **Myth Busting** (400+ reactions)
   - "The myth of X"
   - "X isn't what you think"
   - Debunks belief

4. **Bold Philosophy** (350+ reactions)
   - Life wisdom
   - Universal truth statement
   - "The key to X is..."

5. **Authority/Resource Promise** (350+ reactions)
   - "I spent 100+ hours researching"
   - "After analyzing X..."
   - Credibility claim

6. **Data Teaser** (350+ reactions)
   - "23× better conversion"
   - Specific metrics
   - Number-driven

7. **Numbered List Promise** (300+ reactions)
   - "10 paradoxes that changed my life"
   - "7 habits of..."
   - List format

8. **Personal Vulnerability** (300+ reactions)
   - "I failed at X"
   - "I struggled with Y"
   - Honest admission

9. **Bold Prediction** (300+ reactions)
   - "By 2026, X will be dead"
   - Future claim
   - Timeline + outcome

10. **Anti-establishment** (250+ reactions)
    - Challenges power structures
    - Questions authority
    - "The industry doesn't want you to know..."

### Scoring Breakdown

**Pattern Match (0-40 points)**:
- Exact match to top pattern (#1): 40 points
- Match to patterns #2-4: 35 points
- Match to patterns #5-7: 30 points
- Match to patterns #8-10: 25 points
- Weak/partial match: 15 points
- No clear pattern: 0 points

**Curiosity Gap (0-30 points)**:
- Creates immediate "must read more" feeling: 30 points
- Strong intrigue but not urgent: 20 points
- Some curiosity generated: 10 points
- No curiosity gap: 0 points

**Specificity (0-20 points)**:
- Specific numbers/data/examples: 20 points
- Some specificity (general timeframe/category): 10 points
- Vague/generic: 0 points

**Emotional Trigger (0-10 points)**:
- Strong emotional response (fear/excitement/anger/hope): 10 points
- Some emotion: 5 points
- No emotion: 0 points

### Hook Evaluation Example

**Hook**: "9.99% ignore this AI tool. Catastrophic mistake."

**Analysis**:
- Pattern: Data Teaser + Contrarian Hybrid (#2/#6)
- Pattern Score: 35/40
- Curiosity: "What tool? What mistake?" = 28/30
- Specificity: 9.99% = 18/20
- Emotion: "Catastrophic" = fear trigger = 9/10
- **Total**: 90/100 → High viral prediction

## Evaluation 2: Creator Archetype Alignment (0-100)

Identifies which successful archetype the content follows and scores execution.

### The 6 Archetypes

**1. The Authority** (Allie K. Miller - 1.5M followers)
- **Formula**: Authority Statement → Resource Promise → Framework/List → Access Instructions → Share Prompt
- **Tone**: Professional, educational, heavy visuals
- **Length**: 200-400 words
- **Characteristics**: External links, data-driven, framework focus

**2. The Contrarian** (Peter Yang - 134K followers)
- **Formula**: Contrarian Hook → 3 Counter-Points → Personal Take → Question → Resource Link
- **Tone**: Conversational, always 3 points, link in comments
- **Length**: 150-250 words
- **Characteristics**: Challenges assumptions, numbered structure

**3. The Entertainer** (Elena Verna - 158K followers)
- **Formula**: Bold Statement → Visual/Meme → Product Angle → Hashtag
- **Tone**: <100 words, 60% visual content, strong opinions
- **Length**: 50-100 words
- **Characteristics**: Strategic emojis, image-first

**4. The Educator** (Kieran Flanagan - 96K followers)
- **Formula**: Hook → Context (2-3 lines) → Value (list/framework) → Takeaway → Question
- **Tone**: Data-driven, short paragraphs, ends with question
- **Length**: 200-300 words
- **Characteristics**: Practical value, clear structure

**5. The Philosopher** (Sahil Bloom - 657K followers)
- **Formula**: Number Hook → Paradox/Principle List → Brief Explanation → Personal Application → Newsletter CTA
- **Tone**: Life wisdom, emotional resonance, visual poetry
- **Length**: 150-300 words
- **Characteristics**: Paradoxes, universal truths

**6. The Rebel Leader** (Justin Welsh - 777K followers)
- **Formula**: Common Belief → Why It's Wrong → Personal Evidence → Alternative Path → Course Link
- **Tone**: Anti-establishment, vulnerability + success, story arc
- **Length**: 200-400 words
- **Characteristics**: Challenges status quo, personal journey

### Scoring Breakdown

**Archetype Match (0-25 points)**:
- Clear match to one archetype: 25 points
- Hybrid of two archetypes: 20 points
- Elements from multiple archetypes: 15 points
- Weak archetype alignment: 10 points
- No clear archetype: 0 points

**Formula Execution (0-40 points)**:
- All formula elements present and well-executed: 40 points
- Most elements present (80%+): 32 points
- Some elements present (60%+): 24 points
- Few elements (40%+): 16 points
- Minimal formula adherence: 8 points

**Voice Consistency (0-20 points)**:
- Perfect voice match for archetype: 20 points
- Strong voice consistency: 15 points
- Some voice alignment: 10 points
- Weak voice match: 5 points
- Voice conflicts with archetype: 0 points

**Length Optimization (0-15 points)**:
- Within optimal range for archetype: 15 points
- Slightly off (±20%): 12 points
- Moderately off (±40%): 8 points
- Significantly off (±60%+): 4 points
- Way off range: 0 points

### Archetype Evaluation Example

**Content**: 180-word post with "Common belief → Why wrong → Evidence → Alternative" structure

**Analysis**:
- Primary Archetype: Rebel Leader (Justin Welsh)
- Archetype Score: 23/25
- Formula Execution: Elements present (belief, wrong, evidence, alternative) = 32/40
- Voice: Anti-establishment tone maintained = 18/20
- Length: 180 words (target 200-400) = slightly short = 12/15
- **Total**: 85/100 → Strong archetype alignment

## Evaluation 3: Engagement Mechanics (0-100)

Analyzes triggers for saves, shares, and comments.

### Scoring Breakdown

**Save Triggers (0-25 points)**:
What makes people bookmark to reference later?
- Paradigm shift explanation: +8
- Quotable wisdom/framework: +7
- Specific tactics/process: +5
- Data visualization: +5
- Total: 25 points max

**Share Triggers (0-25 points)**:
What makes people share with their network?
- Contrarian take (professionally safe): +8
- Bold claim with data backing: +7
- Future prediction: +5
- Pattern/insight revelation: +5
- Total: 25 points max

**Comment Triggers (0-20 points)**:
What makes people comment?
- Direct question at end: +7
- "Agree/disagree?" debate: +6
- Decision input request: +4
- Personal story prompt: +3
- Total: 20 points max

**Visual Optimization (0-15 points)**:
Is it visually scannable?
- Line breaks every 2-3 sentences: +5
- Bullet points or numbered lists: +4
- Bold for emphasis: +3
- Image/visual included: +3
- Total: 15 points max

**CTA Effectiveness (0-15 points)**:
Does it have clear next step?
- Specific, actionable CTA: +8
- Soft CTA (question/engagement): +5
- Multiple CTAs (avoid): -3
- No CTA: +0
- Total: 15 points max

### Engagement Evaluation Example

**Content**: Post with contrarian take, data backing, ends with "Agree or disagree?", has bullet list

**Analysis**:
- Save Triggers: Paradigm shift (8) + quotable (7) = 15/25
- Share Triggers: Contrarian (8) + bold claim (7) = 15/25
- Comment Triggers: Debate question (6) = 6/20
- Visual: Line breaks (5) + bullets (4) = 9/15
- CTA: Soft engagement CTA (5) = 5/15
- **Total**: 50/100 → Needs improvement (add direct question, improve visual formatting)

## Composite Analysis

### Calculation

**Example Scores**:
- Viral Hook: 90/100
- Creator Archetype: 85/100
- Engagement Mechanics: 72/100

**Weighted Composite**:
(90 × 0.40) + (85 × 0.25) + (72 × 0.35)
= 36 + 21.25 + 25.2
= **82.45/100**

**Recommendation**: High quality → Publish with confidence

## Quick Scoring Reference

**What makes a 90+ score?**
- Hook matches top 3 viral patterns
- Clear archetype with all formula elements
- Multiple engagement triggers present
- Strong visual formatting
- Specific, compelling CTA

**What causes <50 scores?**
- Generic hook (no pattern match)
- Confused archetype (trying to be everything)
- No clear save/share/comment triggers
- Wall of text (no formatting)
- Missing or unclear CTA

**Fast improvement tactics**:
1. Hook: Add specific number or contrarian angle
2. Archetype: Pick ONE and execute formula fully
3. Engagement: End with direct question
4. Visual: Add bullets and line breaks
5. CTA: Make it specific and actionable
